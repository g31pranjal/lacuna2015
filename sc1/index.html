The Ishbal Ruins

After trekking his way across vast expanses of Hohenheim, Aragon finally made his way to the legendary ruins of Ishbal. He enters the ruins and finds a few doors, most of which are closed. While exploring the open but unknown land, he stumbles onto tabloids. Intriuged by these mystical stones, he decides to unravel its secrets. Will he be able to decipher its meaning? Will he make it past the closed doors to reach the remaining tabloids? Will he be succesful in his endeavour?

Instructions:
1. Use arrow keys for movement.
2. Tabloids are hidden clues which pop up in the right when you step on it.
3. The inner doors are opened after solving the outer clues.
