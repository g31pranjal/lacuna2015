The Mausoleum at Olympus Mons

Olympus Mons has never featured in history books of Hohenheim. Aragon decides to find out the reason behind the absence of the thousands of adventurers gone missing in the ghastly and foreboding depths of the great mountain. A long and uneventful journey finally brings Aragon to an unheard of crematorium. He sees a series of stairways and decides to use them to map out this fiery terrain and solve its mysteries. Help Aragon navigate the mausoleum and find the hidden secrets of Olympus Mons.

Instructions:
1. Use arrow keys for movement.
2. Find and solve hidden clues (they pop up in the right when you step on it).
3. Click on any stairway to alter its orientation and make your way.
